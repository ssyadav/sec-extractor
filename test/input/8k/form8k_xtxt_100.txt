<b>Item 5.07</b>	 <b>Submission of Matters to a Vote ofSecurity
Holders</b></p>
<p style=
"font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; background-color: white">
&nbsp;</p>
<p style=
"font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; background-color: white">
SWK Holdings Corporation (the “Company”)held its 2018 annual
meeting of stockholders on May 21, 2018. At the meeting, the
Company’s stockholders elected MichaelWeinberg, D. Blair Baker,
Christopher W. Haga, and Edward B. Stead, to serve as directors for
a term expiring in 2019. Additionally,stockholders voted to ratify
the selection of BPM, LLP as the Company’s independent registered
public accounting firm forthe fiscal year ending December 31, 2018.
Stockholders also voted affirmatively on an advisory basis to
approve the compensationto certain executives.</p>
