<p STYLE=

ALIGN="center"><b>Explanatory Note</b></p>
<p STYLE=
"margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">
This Amendment No. 1 on Form 8-K/A (this “Amendment”) amends the
Current Report on Form 8-K dated May 21, 2018 (the “OriginalFiling”
) filed by Cadence Bancorporation (the “Company”), providing
audited financial statements and interim unaudited financial
statements of State Bank Financial Corporation (“State Bank”)
relating to the merger of theCompany and State Bank. The purpose of
this Amendment is to (i) add the report of Dixon Hughes Goodman LLP
relating to the Internal Control over Financial Reporting of State
Bank as of December 31, 2017 that was inadvertently omitted from
theOriginal Filing and (ii) to file as an exhibit the consent of
Dixon Hughes Goodman LLP to the incorporation by reference to the
registration statement on Form S-3ASR of the Company (Registration
No. 333-225075) of its reports appearing as exhibitsto this Current
Report on Form 8-K. The full text of the Original Filing is
repeated herein for convenience, but has not been modified from the
text of the Original Filing except solely to include the report and
consent referenced above.</p>
