<p style=
"font-family:'Times New Roman', Times, serif;font-size:10pt;margin:0pt;text-align:justify;text-indent:36pt;">
On May 8, 2018, Saker Aviation Services, Inc. (the “Company”)
approved an expansion to the stock repurchase program authorized on
September 8, 2017, the revision approving the repurchase of up to a
total of 5,000,000 shares of the Company’s outstanding common
stock, par value $0.001 per share (“Common Stock”). Pursuant to
such program, on May 16, 2018, the Company repurchased an aggregate
of 1,783,115 shares of Common Stock from three affiliated
stockholder funds for an aggregate purchase price of approximately
$160,480 ($0.09 per share). The Company’s stock repurchase program
may be suspended, modified or discontinued at any time and the
Company has no obligation to repurchase any additional Common
Stock.</p>
