Nagypal and AmbassadorSiegel are considered independent directors

of five years at $2.50 per share. Mr. Yaspan, in consideration for
serving as Chairmanof the Board, entered into an agreement
providing a one-time grant of 250,000 shares of common stock and a
stock option to acquire250,000 shares of common stock exercisable
for a period of five years at $2.50 per share. Each director will
receive 100,000 sharesof common stock per year vesting in
increments of 25,000 per quarter commencing January 1, 2019. The
Company will also pay Mr.Yaspan, Ms. Nagypal and Ambassador Siegel
